# CueForge Update 002 - Panda Notes

## What changed

CueForge now has a personal UI debugger built into the app.

Right-click any area in the app, tag what felt off, and leave a quick note. The note captures:

- the page
- the clicked area
- the retrieval tag
- the viewport size
- the rough click location
- the developer note

It is built for the little stuff that normal bug reports miss: confusing text, awkward spacing, broken-feeling buttons, missing feedback, slow flows, and "this made sense in your head but not on my screen" moments.

## Privacy line

No hidden upload.

Panda Notes stay local in the browser. They only leave the machine if the tester chooses to export a setup pack or redacted report and send it in.
