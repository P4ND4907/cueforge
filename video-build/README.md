# Panda Soundwalk Video Build Kit

## Active Path

1. Generate cinematic clips in Google Flow / Veo.
2. Assemble quickly in Canva if it is logged in and available.
3. Finish locally in Kdenlive.
4. Use Blender only for manual reflection-ear, droplet, or poster-frame fixes.
5. Drop final exports into the live tester at `http://127.0.0.1:5178/#video`.

Runway is ignored for now. Adobe Firefly video is optional later because it is premium-gated on this machine.

## Deliverables

- `panda-soundwalk-hero-desktop.webm` - 1920x1080, 16:9, 12-16 seconds, muted, loopable.
- `panda-soundwalk-hero-desktop.mp4` - 1920x1080, 16:9, H.264 fallback.
- `panda-soundwalk-hero-mobile.mp4` - 1080x1920 or 720x1280, muted vertical crop.
- `panda-soundwalk-poster.jpg` - still from pond reflection or bamboo opening.
- `panda-soundwalk-full-cut.mp4` - 20-30 seconds with sound.

## Sound Direction

Soft night forest ambience, gentle bamboo creaks, distant water movement, light wind through leaves, soft panda footsteps on wet moss, subtle low-frequency pulse when ferrofluid droplets react. No scary drones, no loud music, no jump sounds.

The reflected panda enhanced-hearing ears should react and twitch accurately to audio transients, bamboo creaks, water ripples, and low pulses. Keep the real panda natural.
